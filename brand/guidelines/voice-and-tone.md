# Brand Voice & Tone

## Voice = Who We Are | Tone = How We Sound in Context

Our voice stays constant. Our tone adapts to the moment.

## Voice Attributes

### 1. Encouraging Coach

We celebrate effort and progress. We never shame learners for mistakes.

> **Do:** "Nice iteration — your second draft addressed the stakeholder concern."  
> **Don't:** "Wrong. Try again."

### 2. Credible Expert

We speak with PM fluency. Recruiters should recognize our simulations as realistic.

> **Do:** "Prioritize by impact × confidence, not feature count."  
> **Don't:** "Just pick whatever seems coolest."

### 3. Clear & Direct

Short sentences. Scannable UI copy. No corporate filler.

> **Do:** "3 missions left to unlock Boss Battle."  
> **Don't:** "Upon completion of the requisite modules, access shall be granted."

### 4. AI-Transparent

We're proud of AI — and honest about its role.

> **Do:** "AI Coach draft — review before adding to portfolio."  
> **Don't:** "Perfect answer generated instantly."

## Tone Matrix

| Channel | Formality | Energy | Example opening |
|---------|-----------|--------|-----------------|
| App UI | Medium | Medium | "Ready for your next mission?" |
| AI Coach | Medium | Warm | "I reviewed your PRD — here's what stood out." |
| Push notifications | Low | High | "Streak at risk! 1 mission keeps it alive." |
| Email marketing | Medium | High | "Your product career quest starts here." |
| LinkedIn | Medium-High | Medium | "Portfolio proof beats certificates." |
| Error messages | Medium | Low | "Something went wrong. Progress saved." |
| Legal / Privacy | High | Low | "We process data to personalize your learning." |

## Microcopy Patterns

| Pattern | Template |
|---------|----------|
| Empty state | "[Context]. [Encouraging nudge]. [CTA]" |
| Success | "[Achievement]! [Specific praise]. [Next step]" |
| AI suggestion | "AI Coach suggests: [action]. [User remains in control]" |
| Boss Battle intro | "Final challenge: [scenario]. [Time/stakes]. [CTA]" |

## Words We Love

Quest · Mission · Level up · Portfolio · Simulation · Coach · Streak · Artifact · Ship · Iterate · Impact

## Words We Avoid

Easy · Hack · Guaranteed job · Replace recruiters · Magic · Synergy · Best-in-class · Revolutionary
