# 1. Purpose

This document explains the unique value PM Quest AI delivers to aspiring Product Managers and AI Product Managers.

It defines why users should choose PM Quest AI instead of using multiple disconnected learning platforms.

---

# 2. Value Proposition Statement

PM Quest AI is an AI-powered gamified learning platform that helps aspiring Product Managers and AI Product Managers become interview-ready through immersive learning, realistic product simulations, personalized AI coaching, and portfolio generation.

Unlike fragmented learning platforms, PM Quest AI combines everything into one engaging ecosystem.
