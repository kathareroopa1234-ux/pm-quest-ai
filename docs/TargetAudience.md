# 1. Purpose

The purpose of this document is to clearly define the users PM Quest AI is designed for.

A deep understanding of the target audience enables better product decisions, prioritization, user experience design, feature development, marketing strategy, pricing, and long-term product growth.

This document serves as the foundation for all future product decisions.

---

# 2. Why Target Audience Matters

Building a product without understanding its users often leads to unnecessary features, poor user experience, weak engagement, and low adoption.

Understanding users allows the product team to:

- Solve real problems instead of assumed problems

- Prioritize high-impact features

- Design intuitive user experiences

- Improve user retention

- Increase interview success rates

- Build a product users genuinely enjoy
