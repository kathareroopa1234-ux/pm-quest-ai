# 1. Purpose

The purpose of this document is to understand the Product Management learning market, AI Product Management education, interview preparation industry, and the opportunities for PM Quest AI.

---

# 2. Executive Summary

The demand for Product Managers and AI Product Managers is growing rapidly as companies adopt AI-powered products and digital transformation.

However, aspiring Product Managers still rely on fragmented learning resources spread across multiple platforms.

PM Quest AI addresses this gap by combining learning, simulations, AI coaching, portfolio building, and interview preparation into a single platform.

# 3. Industry Overview

## Product Management Education

Growing demand due to digital transformation and increasing focus on product-led organizations.

---

## AI Product Management

---

## EdTech

Online learning continues to expand as learners seek flexible, personalized, and career-focused education.

---

## Interview Preparation

Candidates increasingly prefer mock interviews, AI feedback, and practical simulations over traditional learning methods.
